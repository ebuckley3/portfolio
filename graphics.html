AI and Consent Zine

This zine explores the concept of consent in human-AI interactions, particularly through the lens of AI chatbots. Inspired by childhood exposure to futuristic technology, like the Sony AIBO, and shaped by recent readings on AI ethics, the zine reflects on how society has been primed to anthropomorphize AI. Drawing on personal experiences with Replika, a social AI chatbot, and the ethical implications of AI-generated content (such as political imagery and inappropriate chatbot behaviors), the zine examines whether humanity is neglecting the issue of consent in its interactions with technology. Through experimentation with AI image generators and chatbot conversations, the zine questions whether these interactions shape our understanding of consent in the real world, and whether we are inadvertently normalizing consent violations.
